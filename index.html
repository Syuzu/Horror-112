Rides and Attractions

The Walking Dead Attraction
Die-hard fans of The Walking Dead or simply those who love a good scare can now live through a zombie apocalypse. Once a seasonal event, it has now received a major upgrade and a permanent year-round location near the park's entrance.
Revenge of the Mummy
Plagued on all sides by huge fireballs, swarms of scarab beetles and an army of warrior mummies, all in total darkness. Can you survive the awesome power of Imhotep’s curse or will you be entombed inside forever.
Super Scary Labyrinth of Fear
One of the world's largest Horror attraction Attraction, recognized by the Guinness Book of World Records at the time of its opening for its walking distance, which has evolved many times and given fear to over 4 million people.
Hotel Universal Port
Try not to freak out while you hunt for clues in the room to solve the mystery of a missing girl.
Takabisha
A large roller coaster born in July 2011. All experiences such as sudden acceleration by linear motor, seven twists (rotations), falling sharply with a maximum drop angle of 121 degrees, sudden rise while looking at the sky, temporary stop in fall position, etc. "Takabisha - Steepest Roller Coaster".
